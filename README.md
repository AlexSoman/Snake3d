A 3d version of the classic game Snake!
Controls:
W - Move up (towards black cube)
A: Move left (towards blue cube)
S: Move right (away from blue cube)
D: Move down (away from black cube)
Q: Move towards screen. (away from green cube)
E: Move inwards (towards green cube)
Arrow Keys: Rotates the map.

Your goal is simple, yet difficult. Eat all the apples (red cube) without your head (white cube) hitting the walls of the map or your own body!
The Blue, Green and Black cubes are there to help remeber which direction you are facing!
(Note: Game is extremely difficult).
